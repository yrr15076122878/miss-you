【Project Name and Project Series No.】 2016YFB0100600

【Field】 Motor drive and power electronics assembly

【Recommended by】 中国科学院电工研究所

【Project Introduction】

The increase in power density and efficiency as well as cost reduction of the motor drive system for vehicles is a world-wide problem, which is need to be breaked through urgently. The development of high power density automotive motor controllers by taking advantage of the high temperature, high efficiency and high frequency characteristics of silicon carbide (SiC) devices is considered to be the main solution. Support by the new energy vehicle project of National Key R&D Program, the Institute of Electrical Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences led a research and development team consisting of thirteen units across the country to carry out "the study of basic theory and evaluation methods for SiC devices and systems for high temperature vehicles". To take full advantage of high temperature operation ability of SiC devices and develop high temperature converter systems, the team targets three scientific issues, which are carrier transport mechanism of high temperature SiC chip, multi-physical coupling mechanism of high temperature SiC module packaging, and electromagnetic interference generation and propagation mechanism of high temperature SiC motor controller. The team already breaks through some key technologies, such as carrier transport enhancing technology of SiC device, SiC planar double-sided cooled packaging technology and integration method of SiC motor controller. The team successfully designed and developed a prototype 100 kW SiC motor controller. Verified by authoritative testing organizations, the peak power density of the developed controller is over 37.1 kW/L, the maximum efficiency exceeds 98.6% at 30kHz. The power density, peak efficiency and high-efficiency operation zone of the motor controller was significantly improved. This work has accumulated competitive core technology and valuable R&D experience for China’s motor drive for new-energy vehicle.

【Main results and technological innovation】

Using theoretical analysis, numerical simulation and experimental research, the dynamic and static model including power device, temperature field model of film capacitor core, stray inductance model of capacitor and power device, busbar, motor winding, capacitor and power device are established. The electrical, magnetic, thermal and machine-related basic models of the motor drive controllers such as the ground capacitance model and the controller shell stress field model constitute the theoretical basis for the integrated design of the motor drive controller. In the SiC module, a three-phase 1200 V/300 A module containing 72 SiC chips has been developed, which is the SiC module with the largest number of chips and the highest integration in the published literature. In terms of passive components, a multi-function film capacitor with energy storage, power interconnection, heat dissipation, structural support and mechanical protection was designed and developed. Its ripple current absorption capability was improved by 47%~78%. In the aspect of motor drive controller, the decomposition design method "from system and component" is utilized to replace the assembly method "from component to system". Based on the optimal design goal of the controller, the package design requirements of each component are derived from the top of the controller. By breaking the boundary between passive components, electronic system components, heat sinks, and controller structural support components, the structural multiplexing of each component is realized, and the "system integration to guide component packaging" design method of the vehicle motor drive controller was initially established to maximize space utilization. The full SiC controller prototype EVC020 was developed. Its driving power is 85kW, the total volume 2.29L, and the highest efficiency 98.6%@30kHz. The high-efficiency area is 95%, which is 80% more than the silicon-based benchmark prototype. In terms of power density, a substantial improvement from 5 to 8 kW/L to 37.1 kW/L while maintaining high efficiency is realized. In the high-temperature operation research, the peak full-load power test with the inlet water temperature of 105 °C was passed. In the electromagnetic compatibility issue, the conducted emission at high-voltage DC port and the low-voltage power-supply port was tested under full-load operation condition, and complied with class 3 defined in CISPR25.

【Main results and technological innovation】

We are focusing on the frontiers of the fundamental knowledge in Li-ion battery, and the cut-edge technologies and its application. In order to successfully realize the mass production of high-performance and high-safety Li-ion power batteries with specific energy >300Wh/kg, the main technical innovations are shown as follows.
       1.High nickel cathode material with high specific capacity is used in this project. The surface modification of this material will stabilize the material/electrolyte interface, which can enhance the resistance of the material to moisture and carbon dioxide, and also suppress the gas production. This technology can greatly prolong the cycle life and high temperature performance.
       2.A silicon-carbon composite anode material is used.        4.Multi-scale thermal resistance battery cell safety technology. This innovation belongs to the safety technology integration innovation. The key points are:
       (1) using flame retardant electrolyte, positive electrode thermal polymerization additive and composite coating separator to prevent thermal runaway from the battery cell interior;
       (2) the application of thermal resistance materials and the ceramization conversion material to wrap the battery cells to suppress the thermal runaway between the battery cells;
       (3) protecting the battery cell from thermal runaway in three aspects, and these are the novel designs of cathode/anode material particles, the electrode, and the battery cells.

【Field】 Pure electric power system

【Recommended by】 TGOOD New Energy Co., Ltd.

【Project Introduction】

With "two-layer security protection technology for new energy vehicles", TGOOD has established 19 safety models for charging network big data, for the first time breaking the traditional concept that the safety of EV batteries depend entirely on BMS control. TGOOD is the first company in the world, who has not only proposed but also implemented the redundant safety protection of power cells by charging power grids. The scope of protection has been extended to the charging side, the energy side, and the user side, and in terms of time extension of battery lifecycle has been realized. A multi-angle, multi-dimensional, systematic electric vehicle charging safety model is constructed, and the learning and evolution of large data in the cloud are carried out, so that the charging process and car safety hazards can be monitored, early warned, controllable, and traceable.

【Main results and technological innovation】

Based on the scientific big data model, with the active protection of new energy vehicles and the big data monitoring, TGOOD has developed and established a two-layer security protection system: active protection from CMS (flexible intelligent Charging Management System) and the big data security protection system.
CMS Active Protection System: TGOOD has established 11 protection models against charging voltage mismatch, controller communication failure, battery overheating, battery low temperature, battery temperature abnormity, and battery overcharging for each charging process, which will be monitored, analyzed and safety assessed in 27 dimensions. While the BMS (Battery Management System) controls the charging behavior of the vehicle, the CMS will perform double check. When an abnormity is found, active protection will start to terminate the charging task in time to protect the safety of the vehicle.
Big data security protection system: it is a second-tier security protection system established above CMS and based on big data, covering 8 types of protection models. Each charging data will be calculated and analyzed on the big data platform through 19 models with two layers of protection. If the threshold value for protection is reached, charging process will be stopped. In this way, the safety of each EV can be scored, safety hazards of the battery can be accurately identified and warned, also the battery health status and lifespan can be accurately predicted.
